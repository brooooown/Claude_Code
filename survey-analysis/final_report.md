# Mercari UX Research Survey - Comprehensive Analysis Report

## Executive Summary

This report analyzes a comprehensive UX research survey conducted by Mercari from March 12-13, 2026, with **1,656 complete responses** (100% completion rate) for interview participant recruitment. The survey focused on user behavior patterns, feature usage, and shopping preferences across digital platforms.

## Feature Usage Patterns

### "いいね!" (Like) Tab Engagement
- **65.1% Use daily** (1,078 users) - *Extremely high engagement*
- **21.7% Use every 2-3 days** (359 users)
- **7.0% Use weekly** (116 users)
- **Only 1.9% rarely use** - *Strong feature adoption*

### Search Condition Saving Feature
- **High adoption rate** among active users
- Primary use cases:
  - Targeted product hunting (specific items)
  - Monitoring for missed purchase opportunities
  - Convenience/shortcut for repeated searches
  - Seller research and price monitoring

### Follow Feature Adoption
- **Strong correlation** between buyer activity and follow behavior
- Key motivations:
  - **Trust-based relationships** with quality sellers
  - **Taste alignment** for personal style/interests
  - **Convenience** for repeat purchasing
  - **Discovery** of new products from trusted sources
